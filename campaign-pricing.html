Campaign Pricing Model

Costs are split among locals using a ticketing system, so everyone contributes and benefits together. Our milestone-based pricing ensures transparency and fairness for

Milestones & Deliverable Examples

  • $500 — Research and drafting of a submission
  • $1,000 — Research and representation at Council
  • $2,000 — Ongoing comprehensive community representation

How Ticketing Works

  • Each campaign sets a ticket price (e.g., $50 per ticket).
  • Every ticket purchased goes towards reaching the next milestone.
  • Once enough tickets are sold to reach a milestone, consultants begin work on that deliverable.

Example

If 25 tickets are purchased at $50 each, the total raised is $1,250. This covers the first two milestones ($500 + $1,000 = $1,500), but since only $1,250 was raised, the second milestone is partially funded. If more tickets are sold and the total exceeds the milestone cost, any excess is refunded equally among all ticket holders.

For example, if $1,250 is raised and the last milestone was $1,000, the extra $250 is refunded:
$250 / 25 tickets = $10 refund per ticket holder.

Refunds & Fairness

  • Any funds raised above the milestone cost are refunded equally to all ticket holders.
  • This ensures everyone pays only their fair share for each deliverable.
